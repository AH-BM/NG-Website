About Cars

What is a car?

A car, also known as an automobile, is a wheeled motor vehicle used for transportation. Most cars are designed to run primarily on roads, have seating for one to eight people, typically have four wheels, and are constructed mainly for the transport of people rather than goods. They are powered by internal combustion engines or electric motors and come in various shapes, sizes, and styles to suit different needs and preferences.
